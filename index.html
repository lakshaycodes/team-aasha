ABOUT US

'No one is useless in this world who lightens the burden of it to anyone else.'
We, at Team Aasha believe that as humans, it is our sole responsibility to speak up for the right and despise the wrong. We are an organisation of mostly high-school students, who thought of coming together and forming this group for being a voice for everyone.
We believe that it is for the benefit of everyone to have a platform where they can be heard and understood, and thus we aim to provide such healthy surrounding.

As a Indian, I have no duty to be cheated or to allow myself to be cheated, But I have the duty to be fighter for Truth and Justice
